October 19, 2024

The Lord is My Refuge and Strength

During times of chaos, confusion, and calamity we all need a Savior who provides a place of refuge, strength, and safety. During the times I’ve felt afraid and confused, I’ve had to lean into the Lord as my Refuge and Strength.

The prayer below is a prayer I wrote when I needed to remind myself of Whom I can call on in times of trouble. Use it to remind yourself of who God is for you in times of trouble. Then use the scriptures to meditate on and declare the promises of God over your life.

Prayer

I love You Lord. Thank You for hearing my cry. I trust Your Word that says that they that wait upon You will constantly be refreshed and renewed. I will wait on You, Lord.

Scripture Verses

"Wait on the Lord; Be of good courage, And He shall strengthen your heart; Wait, I say, on the Lord!"

Psalm 27:14

"I love the Lord, because He has heard My voice and my supplications."

Psalm 116:1

"But those who wait on the Lord shall renew their strength; They shall mount up with wings like eagles, they shall run and not be weary, they shall walk and not faint."

Isaiah 40:31

October 20, 2024

The Lord is My Provider

Throughout my life, the Lord has proven Himself to be My Provider. In times of loss, transition, or need, God has always been faithful to provide all that I have needed.

I wrote the following prayer to align my heart with Whom God says He is as my Provider. Use the prayer to remind yourself that God is the Ultimate Provider. Then use the scriptures to meditate on and declare the promises of provision over your life

Prayer

Lord I trust You. I trust that You will provide for my every need. I give You praise for what You have done and are about to do in my life. I thank You for Your faithfulness.

Scripture Verses

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways acknowledge Him, and He shall direct your paths."

Proverbs 3:5-6

"Lord, you are my God; I will exalt You and praise Your name, for in perfect faithfulness You have done wonderful things, things planned long ago."

Isaiah 25:1 NIV

"As the deer pants for the water brooks, so pants my soul for You, O God."

Psalm 42:1
